Our Story in Kuala Lumpur

Founded out of a love for traditional Thai desserts, we bring the taste of Bangkok to Kuala Lumpur. Our mango sticky rice is prepared fresh daily using handpicked mangos and fragrant coconut-infused rice.

We started serving friends and neighbors, and soon word spread across the city. Today we deliver to homes and offices throughout the Klang Valley while staying true to our family recipe.
