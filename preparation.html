Preparation Method for Kuala Lumpur Delivery

Our process begins with soaking glutinous rice overnight before steaming it to perfection. Fresh coconut milk is simmered with a touch of sugar and salt to create a rich sauce.

Ripe Thai mangos are sliced just before delivery to ensure maximum freshness. The warm sticky rice is gently mixed with the coconut sauce, then paired with juicy mango slices for a heavenly combination.
